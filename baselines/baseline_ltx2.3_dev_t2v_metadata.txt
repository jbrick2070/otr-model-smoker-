For an actual 5080-appropriate LTX 2.3 run, use the GGUF-quantized variants already on disk instead: unet/ltx-2.3-22b-dev-Q3_K_M.gguf or unet/ltx-2.3-22b-dev-Q4_K_S.gguf (via UnetLoaderGGUF from ComfyUI-GGUF), or the distilled-1.1 GGUF variants under unet/distilled-1.1/.
